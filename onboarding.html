MakanLah is a photo-first meal diary designed for patients and their dietitians. Log meals with a photo, jot down how you felt, and share a structured clinical report — no calorie counting required.

Open the App How it works
Who is this for
Patients
Log what you eat without the hassle of counting calories. Just snap, tag, and write a note.
Dietitians
Receive a structured photo diary report with meal context and patient journal notes, ready to review.
How it works
1
Snap a photo of your meal
Use your phone camera directly in the app, or pick an existing photo from your gallery. No need to type food names — the photo does the talking.
📷 Log tab
2
Tag the context
Select your meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack, or Supper), where you ate, how you were feeling, and the time. Just a few taps — no typing needed.
🏷️ Meal type · Location · Mood
3
Write your journal note
Visit the Journal tab anytime to write how a meal made you feel — before, during, or after. Cravings, fullness, discomfort, emotions — anything that helps paint a fuller picture.
📓 Journal tab
4
Generate your clinical report
When ready, head to the Report tab to produce a structured day-by-day photo diary with all your meal context and notes. Share it with your dietitian or save it as a PDF.
📄 Report tab · Save as PDF
What MakanLah is (and isn't)
Simple by design.
  • ✕ No calorie counting or macro tracking — we leave the nutritional analysis to your dietitian.
  • ✕ No food database to scroll through — photos are faster and more accurate than searching for "nasi lemak biasa."
  • ✕ No account required — your data stays on your device, always.
  • ✓ A structured, shareable record that gives your dietitian real behavioural and contextual insight.
  • ✓ Built for the Malaysian food context — from warung lunches to late-night mamak suppers.
Your data stays on your device.
MakanLah stores everything — photos, notes, and meal logs — directly on your phone or browser using IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded to any server. You choose when to share your report, and with whom.
